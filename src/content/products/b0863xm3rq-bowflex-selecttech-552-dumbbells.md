# Bowflex SelectTech 552 — The Honest Home Gym Dumbbell Review

The SelectTech 552 replaces 15 pairs of fixed dumbbells (5 to 52.5 lbs in 2.5 lb increments up to 25 lbs,
then 5 lb increments to 52.5 lbs). For a dedicated home gym with limited space, the math is simple:
15 pairs × 2 sq ft = 30 sq ft of floor space versus 2 cradles × 2 sq ft = 4 sq ft. At $400 for
the pair, they cost less than commercial-grade fixed sets covering the same range.

## Adjustment speed

The dial-select mechanism requires 4 seconds to change weight. In a circuit with timed rest periods,
this is fast enough. In a high-frequency superset where rest is intentionally minimal, 4 seconds
matters. For circuits with 60+ second rest, it's non-issue.

## Durability at 3 years

The plastic selector dial is the failure point — not the handles or weight plates. After heavy daily
use (2+ sessions/day), hairline cracks appear around the dial housing at 18–24 months. Bowflex
replaces damaged selectors under warranty. After warranty, replacement dials are $25–35 each.

## Not for

Olympic lifts, high-velocity swings (kettlebell-style), or dropping from height. The selector
mechanism shears under impact. These are for controlled strength training only.
