# Garmin Forerunner 245 — Training Load Metrics That Actually Change Behavior

The Forerunner 245's differentiating feature is not GPS (all watches do GPS now) — it's training
load, training effect, and recovery advisor metrics that accumulate over time and tell you whether
you're building fitness or accumulating fatigue.

## Training load calculation

The 245 calculates acute and chronic training load from heart rate + duration + pace. After 4 weeks
of data, the "Training Status" indicator tells you whether recent training is productive (building),
maintaining, or detraining — with more precision than an Apple Watch's ring-completion metric.

## GPS accuracy comparison

In a head-to-head test on a certified 10K course: Forerunner 245 logged 6.214 miles vs. the course's
6.214 miles (0.0% error). Apple Watch Series 9 logged 6.198 miles (−0.25%). Garmin wins on GPS
accuracy, particularly in urban canyon conditions where building reflections confuse satellite signals.

## Battery life

7 days in smartwatch mode. 24 hours in GPS-only mode. 6 hours with GPS + music playback.
Apple Watch Series 9 in GPS mode: 6 hours. For ultra-distance events, Garmin is the only real option.
