# GYMLEDGER Style Kit

## Brand Summary
GYMLEDGER is an app and content brand for tracking lifts, accessory work, and macros. The identity is numbers-first, high-contrast, and serious without influencer gloss.

**Tagline:** Lift tracking without the noise.

## Color Palette
| Role | Hex |
|---|---|
| primary | #E63946 |
| secondary | #0B0C10 |
| accent | #6C727C |
| background | #F4F4F1 |
| text | #0B0C10 |
| muted | #8A8D91 |

Body-text contrast: `#0B0C10` on `#F4F4F1` = **17.74:1**.

## Type Pairing Recommendation
Wordmark/display: condensed industrial sans such as Bebas, Druk, or Roboto Condensed Black. UI/body: Inter or Roboto Mono for logged data.

## Voice & Tone Notes
Direct, numbers-first, anti-influencer, and concise. Say what matters: sets, reps, load, trend, recovery.

## Do
- Use monochrome contrast, ledger grids, and one sharp signal accent.
- Make CTAs short and functional.
- Keep layouts disciplined and data-forward.

## Don't
- Do not use neon gym-club styling or dudebro slogans.
- Do not make the interface feel cluttered.
- Do not use wellness fluff when a number will do.
