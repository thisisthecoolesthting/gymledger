Resistance Loop Bands — The Most Underused Tool in Home Gyms

Resistance loop bands weigh 2 oz each and cost $14 for a set of 5. They handle glute activation warm-up, hip abduction strengthening, assisted pull-up progressions, and banded push-ups for additional resistance. For what they do, nothing else matches the value.

Tension ratings tested

We used a force gauge to measure tension at 50% stretch:

  • Light (yellow): 8 lbs
  • Medium (green): 14 lbs
  • Heavy (blue): 22 lbs
  • X-Heavy (black): 32 lbs
  • XX-Heavy (purple): 44 lbs

These align with the labeled ratings. For glute activation (clamshells, monster walks), medium is the standard starting point. For assisted pull-ups, X-Heavy or XX-Heavy bands loop over the bar.
